Penne Rustica: A Delicious Italian Dish

Description

Penne Rustica is an Italian-American dish popularized by the Macaroni Grill chain restaurant. The dish is a creamy concoction of pasta, meat, and seasoning. If you are a fan of rich Italian-American dishes, this is a must try.

Ingredients

For the Sauce:

  • 2 teaspons of butter
  • 2 teaspons of chopper garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon of dill
  • 1 teaspoon of chopped rosemary
  • 1 cup marsala wine
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 8 cups of heavy cream

For the Pasta:

  • 1 ounce of bacon or pancetta
  • 18 peeled shrimp
  • 12 ounce of sliced, grilled chicken breasts
  • 20 ounces of penne pasta noodles
  • 3 teaspoons of chopped pimiento
  • 1 teaspoon of chopped shallot
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 cup of shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 6 sprigs of fresh rosemary

Instructions

  1. For the Sauce, saute the butter, garlic and rosemary until garlic begins to brown.
  2. Add wine and reduce by one-third.
  3. Add remaining sauce ingredients and set to the side.
  4. Now, for the pasta side of the recipe, saute bacon or pancetta until it begins to brown.
  5. Add butter, shallots and shrimp.
  6. Cook until shrimp is evenly pink.
  7. Add chicken, salt, pepper and mix all together.
  8. Finally, add sauce alongside 1/2 cup of the parmesan cheese.
  9. Let dish simmer until the sauce thickens.
  10. Combine the shrimp and chicken with the pasta in a large bowl.
  11. Top with remaining parmesan cheese, as well as pimientos and paprika.
  12. Bake at 475 degrees fahrenheit for 10 to 15 minutes.
  13. garnish with rosemary sprig.
