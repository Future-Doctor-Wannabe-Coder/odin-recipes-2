Papas Fritas: A classic side dish around the world

Description

Papas Fritas, otherwise known as french fries, a common side dish served alongside main dishes throughout the world, particularly in North America, South America, and Europe. They're best prepared crispy, salted, and alongside a condiment.

Ingredients

  • Medium Russet Potatos
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Optional: shredded cheeses, spices, seasonings and Condiments of Choice

Instructions

  1. Cut the potatoes into large rectangular pieces using a knife.
  2. Boil those pieces until they become slighly bendy.
  3. Lay them in a single layer on a baking sheet, and soak them in oil.
  4. Next, bake them at 450 degrees fahrenheit for roughly 45 minutes.
  5. Remove the fries, flip them over, add more oil, and cook for 20 more minutes, or until ready.
  6. Finish by adding salt, as well as any kind of shredded cheese, spices, or seasoning.
  7. Serve alone or alongside condiment of choice.
